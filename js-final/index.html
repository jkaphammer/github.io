Phone Snake

Hi! Use the arrow keys to move the snake over the numbers you want. When he eats a number, it will be added to the phone number form. When you are done entering your phone number, make the snake eat the submit button! Watch out, if you mess up, you will have to either hit a wall or make the snake eat himself to reset.
